Hello all. My name is Hong-Chul Kim, and I am the Head Coach of the SONO FELICE Equestrian Club.

Equestrian sports are becoming more popular every day.
As the demand for healthy lifestyles and high quality leisure activities is increasing, 25,000 people are now engaged in equestrian social clubs in about 300 equestrian clubs across the country.
Today, riding and yachting are the leisure activities that are in the spotlight, and are more prominent even than tennis and golf.

Understanding this shift in the trend, it gives me great pleasure to open SONO FELICE Equestrian Club as a prestigious brand of Dae-Myung's leisure industry.
Our club is a leading equestrian club, equipped with the nation's best facility and coaching groups.
The facility was built by professionals from the design stage, making clients, horses, and maintenance the key priorities in facility arrangement. Also, the club has two indoor arenas and is the first in the nation to prepare an individual paddock for each horse, providing a special space for riders at all levels of experience and interest.
As well, the racetrack floor, constructed with the most current technology using TRANSPOFIX imported from Germany, secures the safety of riders and horses.
As horse-riding is a sport that involves interaction and balance with living creatures, our staff promises to bring you a more enjoyable and beautiful riding experience at SONO FELICE.
