• Riding reservations must be made by phone.
  • We receive horse-riding applications, and arrange for an appropriate coach and horse.
  • Duration of the lesson and the riding can be selected depending on the pass.
  • Refund Policy: 100% refund if cancellation is made by 2 days prior to date/ 50% refund if cancellation is made 1 day prior to date/ same day cancellation or refund is not permitted (for one-day horse riding pass)

  • Jeans or khakis are recommended for riding.
  • Hiking boots or other similar types of shoes cannot be worn.
  • Helmets, chaps, and safety vests are supplied by the club.
  • Personal riding gloves should be carried at all times and used when necessary.

  • SONO FELICE Equestrian Club Hours of Operation: 9:00 AM ~ 5:30 PM
  • Hours of operation are subject to change each season, and are dependent on the conditions of the weather and the individual horses
  • Hours of operation can change due to conditions of the club.
  • We will make an announcement if there is a change in the schedule. Please check our website for further information.

  • Since riding is an activity that requires interaction with horses, we prohibit any type of object that could frighten or upset them.
  • Riding may be restricted if the rider is considered physically or mentally unfit.
  • Smoking is prohibited within the club except in the designated smoking area.
